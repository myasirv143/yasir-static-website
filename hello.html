The Riello Master Plus MPS is an on-line, double-conversion UPS offering total protection for mission-critical applications.
UPS devices have three-phase inputs and one or three-phase outputs. Depending on the power supply requirements, the power range is 3:1 10 to 120 VA or 3:3 10 to 200 VA. The Master Plus MPS is designed to streamline backup power supply from generators and provide seamless start-up. Additionally, it is possible to combine up to eight units in parallel and use a range of battery technologies
